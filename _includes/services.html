Filling & Fairing

Our highly skilled fairing teams will perfect the geometry of your hull and superstructure. Edging the straightest of lines and forming the smoothest of surfaces. Providing the best foundation possible for that mirror finish in the paint to come.

Top Coating

Our master top coat sprayers work together using their time honed experience and techniques to deliver a seamless and uniform paint finish over expansive areas. Applying the most demanding of coatings including high gloss, metallics and iridescents.

Interior Painting

We can take care of all interior corrosion protection works including painting of Engine Rooms, Lockers, Bilges, Tanks and behind Insulation. Whether the surface is visible or hidden the same rigour in application is needed when it comes to protecting your investment.
